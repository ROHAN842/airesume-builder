Q3. List down minimum 1 AI solution for Insurance which you
    will develop for client usage
----------------------------------------------------------------

Policy RAG Assistant — an AI-powered conversational assistant
that allows insurance clients (policyholders and brokers) to
query their own policy documents in plain English and receive
accurate, cited answers grounded strictly in their actual policy
— not generic knowledge.

A client can ask questions like "Am I covered for flood damage?",
"What is my deductible for hospitalization?", or "Does my policy
include third-party liability?" and instantly receive a precise
answer along with the exact clause and page number from their
policy document — available 24/7 without needing to call a
support agent.

Under the hood, the solution uses a LangGraph-based RAG pipeline
where a document ingestion MCP handles PDF parsing and chunking,
a vector store (Chroma / Qdrant) stores semantic embeddings of
policy content, and a retrieval agent fetches the most relevant
clauses before generating a grounded, citation-backed response.
The system is built to handle multiple policy types — health,
motor, property, and commercial — and can be white-labelled for
any insurer using Hexaware's JARVIS platform.

This solution eliminates the most common client pain point in
insurance (not understanding what their policy covers), reduces
inbound support call volume significantly, and gives Hexaware a
repeatable, demonstrable AI asset that can be sold across P&C,
health, and commercial insurance verticals globally.
